10 Ten Facts About National Parks
National parks cover 3.4% of the United States.
Sequoia National Park is home to the largest tree in the world.
Mammoth Cave National Park has the longest cave system in the world.
Great Sand Dunes National Park has the tallest sand dune in North America.
Yellowstone has a flower that grows and can't be found anywhere else in the World.
Hot Springs National Park used to be a spa.
Dry Tortuga is the most remote national park.
Mesa Verde National Park protects 5,000 archaeological sites.
Great Smokey Mountains is the most visited national park.
Crater Lake National Park has America's deepest lake.
